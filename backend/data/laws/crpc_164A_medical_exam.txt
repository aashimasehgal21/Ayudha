CrPC Section 164A — Medical Examination of Rape Victim

1. Mandatory medical examination of victim within 24 hours of receiving information.
2. Examination must be conducted by a registered medical practitioner.
3. Written consent of woman is required.
4. Report must detail injuries, mental state, and biological samples.
5. Report must be forwarded to investigating police officer without delay.
6. Absence of physical injury does NOT disprove rape.
