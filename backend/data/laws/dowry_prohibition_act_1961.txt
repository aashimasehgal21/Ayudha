Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961 — Essentials

1. Giving, taking, or demanding dowry is illegal.
2. Dowry = any valuable property or security exchanged at marriage.
3. Punishment: 5 years imprisonment + fine of ₹15,000 or value of dowry.
4. Demand for dowry (verbal or written) is a punishable offence.
5. Dowry death (Section 304B IPC) linked with this Act: death within 7 years of marriage due to cruelty/dowry demand.
6. Burden of proof often shifts to husband/relatives in dowry death cases.
