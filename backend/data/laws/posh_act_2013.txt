POSH Act, 2013 — Sexual Harassment at Workplace

1. Protects women at workplace (office, factory, remote work, clients, etc.).
2. Defines sexual harassment: unwelcome physical contact, sexual remarks,
   showing pornography, demands for sexual favors.
3. Mandatory Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) for organizations with 10+ employees.
4. Complaints must be filed within 3 months (extendable).
5. Employer must ensure safety, no retaliation, and proper redressal.
6. ICC can recommend termination, wage deductions, written apology, counseling.
7. Local Complaints Committee (LCC) for small workplaces.
