Criminal Law (Amendment) Act, 2013 — Key Points

1. Expanded definition of sexual offences after the 2012 Nirbhaya case.
2. Introduced new crimes: sexual harassment (354A), assault to disrobe (354B),
   voyeurism (354C), stalking (354D).
3. Redefined rape under IPC Section 375: includes penetration of any object/body part.
4. Increased punishment for rape (376): 7 years to life imprisonment.
5. Introduced Section 376A, 376D (gang rape), 376E (repeat offenders).
6. Made acid attack (326A, 326B) a specific punishable offence.
7. Police must register FIR immediately (CrPC Section 154 amendment).
8. Medical examination must follow Section 164A standards.
