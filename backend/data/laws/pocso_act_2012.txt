POCSO Act, 2012 — Protection of Children from Sexual Offences

1. Provides protection to anyone below 18 years from sexual assault,
   sexual harassment, and pornography.
2. Defines different offences: penetrative assault, aggravated assault,
   sexual harassment, use of children for pornographic purposes.
3. Mandatory reporting: any person aware of child sexual abuse must report it.
4. Child-friendly procedures for police, medical exam, and court proceedings.
5. Statement of the child must be recorded sensitively, preferably by female officer.
6. Special courts ensure speedy trial.
