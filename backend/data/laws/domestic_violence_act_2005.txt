Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act (PWDVA), 2005 — Essentials

1. Protects women from physical, emotional, sexual, verbal, and economic abuse.
2. Includes husbands, partners, relatives, and people in domestic relationships.
3. Provides immediate protection orders from court to stop violence.
4. Residence rights: woman cannot be evicted from shared household.
5. Monetary relief: maintenance, medical expenses, damages.
6. Protection Officers must assist in filing complaints and providing shelter.
7. Ex parte (urgent) orders available for immediate safety.
