Medical Examination Procedure for Sexual Assault Survivors (Under CrPC 164A)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Where is examination done?
   - Government hospital
   - Authorized private hospital

2. Consent
   - Mandatory written consent of survivor
   - For minors → consent of guardian

3. Who conducts the exam?
   - A registered female medical practitioner

4. Survivor Rights
   - Free medical treatment
   - No police requisition required
   - No "two-finger test" (banned by Supreme Court)
   - Privacy and confidentiality assured

5. What Examination Includes
   - Physical injuries assessment
   - Sample collection (DNA, swabs)
   - Clothing evidence
   - Emotional and mental state evaluation

6. Medical Report Content
   - Time and date of exam
   - Injuries noted
   - Samples preserved
   - Survivor statement (verbatim)

7. Time Limit
   - Should be done within 24 hours of incident

8. Police Responsibility
   - Escort survivor to hospital
   - Ensure evidence preservation
