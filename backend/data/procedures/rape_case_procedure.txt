Rape Case Procedure (Section 375/376 IPC + CrPC Guidelines)
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Immediate Steps After Assault
   - Ensure safety
   - Do not wash clothes or bathe (preserves evidence)
   - Call 112 or visit hospital

2. Where to Report
   - Nearest Police Station
   - Women Helpline: 1091
   - Emergency Number: 112

3. FIR Registration
   - Mandatory under Section 154 CrPC
   - Female police officer must record statement
   - FIR cannot be refused for jurisdiction issues

4. Medical Examination
   - Conducted within 24 hours
   - No two-finger test allowed

5. Statement Before Magistrate
   - Statement recorded under Section 164 CrPC
   - Child victims get child-friendly procedures (POCSO)

6. Investigation
   - Within 2 months (Fast-track)

7. Witness & Victim Protection
   - Identity confidentiality
   - Escort and protection if threatened

8. Trial & Compensation
   - Fast-track courts for rape cases
   - Compensation under victim scheme
