Stalking Complaint Procedure (Section 354D IPC)
-----------------------------------------------

1. What is Stalking?
   - Following a woman repeatedly
   - Monitoring online activities
   - Persistent calls or messages
   - Showing up at workplace/home repeatedly

2. Where to Report
   - Nearest Police Station
   - Women Helpline 1091
   - Cyber Cell (in case of online stalking)

3. FIR Registration
   - FIR under Section 354D (cognizable)
   - Police must record the complaint immediately

4. Protection Measures
   - Restraining order
   - Police surveillance on accused
   - Emergency protection under DV Act (if domestic relation)

5. Evidence to Collect
   - Call logs, messages
   - CCTV footage
   - Photos/videos
   - Witness statements

6. Punishment
   - Imprisonment up to 3 years (first offence)
   - Higher punishment for repeated offences
