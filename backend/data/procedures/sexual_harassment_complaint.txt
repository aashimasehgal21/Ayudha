Sexual Harassment Complaint Process (Workplace + Public Spaces)
----------------------------------------------------------------

1. Types of Sexual Harassment
   - Unwelcome touching
   - Verbal sexual comments
   - Request for sexual favours
   - Showing pornography
   - Online harassment

2. Where to File Complaint
   Workplace:
     - Internal Complaints Committee (ICC)
     - Police (Sec 354A IPC)

   Public places:
     - Nearest Police Station
     - Women Helpline 1091
     - Cyber complaints (if online)

3. FIR Registration (If IPC offence)
   Offences may include:
     - 354A (sexual harassment)
     - 509 (outraging modesty)
     - 354D (stalking)
     - 354B/C (assault, voyeurism)

4. Evidence to Collect
   - Screenshots, chats, videos
   - Witness details
   - CCTV footage

5. Survivor Rights
   - Protection from retaliation
   - Confidentiality
   - Support person during statement
